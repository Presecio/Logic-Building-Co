What We Do

Solutions Under One Roof

Every trade, every discipline — coordinated seamlessly so you deal with one team, one contract, zero stress.

Bathroom Renovations

Full bathroom transformations from design to completion. Luxury finishes, premium fixtures, waterproofing and tiling — all handled in-house with meticulous attention to detail.

Electrical Works

Licensed electrical installation, upgrades, and compliance work. From new circuits and switchboard upgrades to full rewiring and outdoor lighting.

Plumbing

Complete plumbing services including new installations, repairs, drain solutions, and fixture replacements. Certified plumbers on every job.

Tiling & Waterproofing

Expert tiling across all surfaces — floors, walls, wet areas and outdoor spaces. Full waterproofing systems installed to Australian standards.

Verandahs & Outdoor

Custom-built verandahs, pergolas, and alfresco spaces designed to extend your living area. Built to withstand Melbourne's climate with quality materials.

Insurance Works

Experienced in handling insurance claims and repair work. We liaise directly with insurers, provide detailed reports and restore your property to pre-loss condition.
